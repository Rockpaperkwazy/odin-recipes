Pasta Recipe

Description

A homemade delicousness with the steps to prepare it just for you and maybe a friend (or two).

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Turn the dough out and knead it for ten minutes.
  3. Let the dough rest.
  4. Divide dough into balls.
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes.
